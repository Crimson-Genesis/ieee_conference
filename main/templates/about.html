About Us

Welcome to the IEEE International Conference on Emerging Technologies 2025, proudly hosted by Reva University. This prestigious event serves as a global platform for academics, researchers, and industry professionals to converge and explore the cutting-edge advancements in emerging technologies.

About the Conference

The IEEE International Conference on Emerging Technologies 2025 aims to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing in fields that are shaping the future. The conference will feature keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, workshops, and panel discussions covering topics such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Systems
  • Blockchain and FinTech Innovations
  • Advanced Communication Networks
  • Sustainable and Green Technologies
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Cybersecurity and Privacy

With a vision to bridge the gap between academia and industry, the conference offers an invaluable opportunity to connect with experts, exchange ideas, and explore potential collaborations.

About Reva University

Located in the vibrant city of Bangalore, India, Reva University is a hub for academic excellence and innovation. Renowned for its commitment to fostering research and development, Reva University empowers students and scholars to pursue knowledge and contribute to society through technology and innovation.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, diverse community, and experienced faculty ensure a rich learning and research environment. Hosting this IEEE conference reflects our dedication to advancing technology and building a better future for all.
